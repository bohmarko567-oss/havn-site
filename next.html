FAQ

What is the 4th piece?

STEADY — N°04, a six-botanical liquid formula with a primary direction of 2 ml (40 drops) once daily. On its own it's $18 once, $15 billed monthly, $28 billed every two months, or $39 billed every three months. Each subscribed ritual delivery includes all four: Rise, Calm, Rest and Steady.

How does the subscription work?

Choose a one-, two- or three-month supply. Rise, Calm, Rest and Steady ship together on that cadence: $99 monthly, $190 every two months, or $264 every three months. A one-time ritual is $132. To change or cancel, email hello@havn.co at least 48 hours before renewal. There is no cancellation fee.

Singles or the ritual — what's the actual math?

Three mains on subscription run $93/mo. The complete ritual is $99/mo and carries STEADY too — $6 more for an $18 bottle. Buying all four loose costs $108/mo, so completing the set saves $9 every month — and it stays exactly $9 at every supply length (loose $108/$104/$97 against the ritual's $99/$95/$88). Nothing is a promotion: it's the same four bottles in every delivery, for as long as you stay.

Can I actually cancel anytime?

Yes. Email hello@havn.co at least 48 hours before your next renewal. There is no cancellation fee, and we confirm the change by email.

Shipping & returns?

Orders with a pre-discount delivery subtotal of $50 or more ship free in the contiguous U.S.; smaller orders pay $6.95. The same threshold applies to one-time and subscription deliveries. The first order carries a 30-day product-price guarantee. Full policies.

What results are promised?

HAVN does not promise a specific result or timeline. Use each formula only as directed; the first order carries the stated 30-day product-price guarantee, subject to the limits in the full policy.

Where can I read each label?

Use the product or cart links to open Calm, Rest and Steady's available label panels. Rise opens its audited facts and unresolved label-review status; it is not presented as a final Supplement Facts panel.

Who makes it, and what should I know before use?

The four product records identify the manufacturer's country as the USA. Read each label before use. Consult a physician if you are pregnant, nursing, under 18, taking medication or have a medical condition.
