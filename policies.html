Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

HAVN (“we,” “us”) is operated from Denmark and sells in the United States. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

  • Order info: name, email, shipping/billing address, phone number, and order history — required to process and deliver your order.
  • Payment info: when checkout is live, payment details are handled by Stripe. HAVN does not store full card numbers.
  • Discount-code requests: your email address is used to create, display, validate, and prevent misuse of the requested code. A code request does not enroll you in marketing; that requires separate consent.
  • Site preferences: cart and interface preferences may be stored locally on your device.

Who processes it for us

Providers used for the store include GitHub Pages (current storefront hosting), Vercel (checkout endpoints when deployed), Stripe (payments when checkout is live), our U.S. fulfillment partner (packing and shipping), and Resend (transactional email when configured). Each receives only the information needed for its role. We do not sell personal information.

Your rights

Wherever you live, you can request access, correction, deletion, or a copy of your data, and opt out of marketing at any time: hello@havn.co. EU/EEA residents have GDPR rights (the data controller is HAVN, Denmark); California and other U.S.-state residents may have rights under their state laws.

Retention

Order records are retained as needed for fulfillment, support, accounting, tax, and legal obligations. Discount-code records are retained for validation and abuse prevention. Any separately consented marketing data is retained until you unsubscribe or it is no longer needed.

Refund & Returns Policy

Last updated: July 2026

30-day first-order product-price guarantee

If you request a guarantee refund within 30 days of delivery of your first order, HAVN will refund the eligible product price for up to one unit of each formula. For a two- or three-month first delivery, the guarantee covers one month's unit of each formula at that plan's per-month price, not the entire multi-month subtotal. For example, one Complete Ritual unit is $95 on the two-month plan or $88 on the three-month plan. Email hello@havn.co with your order details. HAVN may ask for additional information or a photo; no return is required unless requested.

  • One guarantee refund per customer / household / payment method — ever. It's a first-try safety net, not a coupon.
  • Shipping charges and units beyond the one-unit-per-formula limit are not refundable under the guarantee. The stated two- and three-month supply plans are allowed; their extra units simply fall outside this first-try guarantee.
  • Approved refunds go to the original payment method. Bank and card-processing times vary.

Subscriptions

The guarantee above applies to the first subscription order, subject to its one-unit-per-formula limit. To stop a future renewal, request cancellation before its billing date and allow time for confirmation. Opened renewals are not eligible for a voluntary return after shipment. Damaged, wrong, or missing items

If anything arrives damaged, incorrect, or short, email your order details and a photo within 14 days of delivery. HAVN will review the issue and provide a replacement or refund when appropriate. This does not count against the first-order guarantee.

Delivery problems

If carrier tracking shows “delivered” but nothing arrived, contact us within 7 days. HAVN will review the order and carrier information and determine an appropriate replacement or refund. If a package is returned because the supplied address was wrong or undeliverable, any reshipment may require new postage.
