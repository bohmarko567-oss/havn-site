Refund & Returns Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Opened products

Our products are dietary supplements — consumables. For safety and quality reasons, opened or used products are not eligible for return or refund, except as described under “Damaged or incorrect items” below.

Unopened products

Unopened, unused products in their original packaging can be refunded within 30 days of delivery. Email hello@havnritual.store with your order number; we’ll confirm whether the item needs to be shipped back. If a return shipment is requested, return shipping is at your expense and the item must arrive in resalable condition. Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–10 business days.

Damaged or incorrect items

If your order arrives damaged, defective, or incorrect, email us within 14 days of delivery with your order number and a photo of the item and its packaging, and we’ll ship a free replacement (or refund, your choice) — no return needed.

Subscriptions

Skip or cancel anytime from your account before your next billing date to avoid a charge. Once a renewal order has been processed and shipped, it is not refundable except under the rules above.

Lost packages & address errors

Orders shipped to an address entered incorrectly at checkout, and packages marked as delivered by the carrier, are not eligible for refund. If the carrier confirms a package was lost in transit, we’ll replace it free of charge.

Fair use

We may decline refunds where we see abuse of this policy (for example, repeated refund claims across multiple orders).
