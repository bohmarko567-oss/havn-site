Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

HAVN (“we,” “us”) is operated from Denmark and sells in the United States. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

What we collect

  • Order info: name, email, shipping/billing address, phone number, and order history — required to process and deliver your order.
  • Payment info: handled entirely by Stripe. Card numbers never touch our servers.
  • Email signups: your address, if you join the list or claim a welcome code.
  • Usage data: we use cookieless, privacy-first analytics (no cross-site tracking, no ad identifiers). Your cart is stored on your own device.

Who processes it for us

Only providers we need to run the store: Stripe (payments), our U.S. fulfillment partner (packing and shipping), Vercel (hosting), Resend (email), and our analytics provider. Each receives only what its job requires. We never sell personal information.

Your rights

Wherever you live, you can request access, correction, deletion, or a copy of your data, and opt out of marketing at any time: hello@havn.co. EU/EEA residents have GDPR rights (the data controller is HAVN, Denmark); California and other US-state residents have equivalent rights under their state laws. We answer within 30 days.

Retention

Order records are kept as long as bookkeeping law requires (5 years under Danish rules), marketing emails until you unsubscribe.

Refund & Returns Policy

Last updated: July 2026

The 30-day “feel it or free” guarantee — first order

Supplements take time, and we ask you to give the ritual a real try. If you're still not feeling it within 30 days of delivery of your first order, email us and we'll refund the product price of that first order (up to one unit of each formula). No need to ship anything back — we may simply ask a couple of questions or for a photo.

  • One guarantee refund per customer / household / payment method — ever. It's a first-try safety net, not a coupon.
  • Shipping charges are not refundable, and the guarantee doesn't apply to orders bought with the intent to stockpile (e.g. multiples of the same formula).
  • Refunds go to the original payment method within 5–10 business days of approval.

Subscriptions

Your first subscription shipment is covered by the guarantee above. After that, the way to stop is to skip or cancel before the next billing date — free, instant, no questions. A renewal that has already shipped can't be refunded once opened; unopened renewals reported within 7 days of billing can be.

Damaged, wrong, or missing items

If anything arrives damaged, incorrect, or short, email a photo within 14 days of delivery and we'll ship a replacement free — usually within 3 business days. This never counts against your guarantee.

Delivery problems

If tracking shows “delivered” but nothing arrived, contact us within 7 days and we'll open a carrier investigation and make it right. If a package returns to us because the address you gave was wrong or undeliverable, we're happy to reship — reshipment postage is on you.
